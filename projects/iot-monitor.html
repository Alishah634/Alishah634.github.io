Project Overview

A comprehensive IoT solution for environmental monitoring that combines custom hardware design with cloud-based analytics and machine learning for predictive insights.

The system monitors temperature, humidity, air quality, and atmospheric pressure in real-time, providing alerts and predictions for environmental changes.
